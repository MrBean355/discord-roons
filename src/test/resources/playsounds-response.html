Playsounds usage

The playsound feature allows viewers to play predefined soundbites on stream.

Playing a sound costs 5000 points.
There is a global cooldown of 5 seconds. Check the table below for the individual sample cooldown as it might be higher for individual samples.

You can play each soundbite by typing the listed command in chat .

Playsounds are currently enabled.
