Playsounds usage

The playsound feature allows viewers to play predefined soundbites on stream.

Playing a sound costs 14000 points.
There is a global cooldown of 15 seconds and a per-user cooldown of 60 seconds. Check the table below for the individual sample cooldown as it might be higher for individual samples.

You can play each soundbite by typing the listed command in chat .

Playsounds are currently enabled.
